XTar - WWWTar

The engine to XTar is also available as a CGI program - WWWTar. With WWWTar installed on your web server, you can expose the contents of tar files to users of your site and they don't have to download the entire archive.

For more information on WWWTar see:

http://www.stella-consulting.ltd.uk/wwwtar/
